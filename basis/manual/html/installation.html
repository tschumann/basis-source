Installation

 

Extract the \basis\ folder from the .zip file and copy it to C:\Program Files\Steam\steamapps\SourceMods\ (or wherever Half-Life 2 is installed) then restart your Steam client. If Basis Source does not show up in your My Games list, restart Steam. Basis will not work if you do not have the Source SDK Base 2007. The Source SDK Base 2007 comes with Half-Life 2, Half-Life 2 Deathmatch (except for ATi and NVIDIA promotions), Half-Life 2 Episode One, Half-Life 2 Episode Two, Counter-Strike Source, Day of Defeat Source, Team Fortress 2, Left 4 Dead and Left 4 Dead 2.
